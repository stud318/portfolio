My Projects

Electrical & Telecommunications

AI-Powered Traffic Control System

Developed an AI-driven traffic control system for a four-way roundabout using a Raspberry Pi 4 as the core controller. GPS modules from each direction feed real-time traffic data into a Transformer AI model, which analyzes traffic density and optimizes the traffic light sequence. The Intelligent Traffic Control system adjusts traffic signals based on volume, effectively streamlining traffic flow and reducing congestion.

AI Grading System

Implemented a sensor-driven pomology grading system that utilizes advanced spectral data and a Random Forest algorithm to assess ripeness in real-time. Fruits are seamlessly directed to their designated grading bins via gear motor systems, ensuring consistent quality and minimizing waste. This efficient AI-powered process enhances the supply chain from farm to market, optimizing productivity and ensuring that only the best produce reaches consumers.
